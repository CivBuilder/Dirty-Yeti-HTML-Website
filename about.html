Our Story

Our shop was founded in 2022 by ski bum Chris Catechis, a rider who is passionate about the sport and the culture.

After years in the tech industry he retired and followed his passions to his next great adventure, Dirty Yeti.

Created for riders by riders

All are accepted here (except skiers) and the purpose of our shop is to perpetuate the culture and deliver premium gear for our patrons,

we believe in the gear we sell; we believed in the gear before we believed in our business.

We do charge extra for shipping as we are committed to ensuring our business is carbon netural, if you aren't about it do business elsewhere.
